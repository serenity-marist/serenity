FAQ

What is serenity?

Serenity is a dashboard created by Marist College seniors to give a simpler and more aesthetic degreeworks page for students to follow their progress.

How does it work?

You need to be a currently enrolled student to use this service. Go to //URL// and click continue to go to the log-in page Input your Marist-login credentials (your marist email account or user name)

Why am I getting a login error message

Make sure you are putting the correct Marist college credential and password If your credentials have expired, call the Marist College Helpdesk to reset your password.

Does Degreasy Store any user data?

Your data is not saved anywhere, it is locally rendered in your browser session and disappears when you close out the page or tab.

Can you save your Degree dashboard?

There is a print option to save your dashboard as a PDF.

Why did we make this?

We wanted to help students understand their degree progress without wading through a complicated degreeworks page, but with a quick glance.

Is this open source?

It is open source free software and will always be free and accessible to Marist Students.
